About RTA Quiz

The RTA Quiz is designed to help individuals prepare for their driving theory tests by providing a variety of questions related to road rules, traffic signs, and safe driving practices. Whether you're a new driver or looking to refresh your knowledge, this quiz offers a fun and interactive way to learn.
